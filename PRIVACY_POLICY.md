# Privacy Policy for Object Measurer

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Introduction

Object Measurer ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Object Measurer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Display name (optional)

### Measurement Data
When you use the App to measure objects, we collect:
- Photos you capture for measurement (processed server-side, then deleted from memory)
- Measurement results (dimensions, unit preferences)
- Calibration session data (reference object type and dimensions)

### Camera Access
The App requires camera access to:
- Capture photos of objects for measurement
- Provide real-time measurement overlays

**We do not record video or audio.** Camera access is used solely for the measurement feature.

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage analytics (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and improve the measurement service
- Store your measurement history
- Authenticate your account
- Process photos for object measurement (photos are processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them)

## Data Storage and Security

- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- Authentication uses JWT tokens
- All API communication uses HTTPS encryption
- Measurement photos are processed and can be deleted upon request

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics for analytics purposes.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your measurement history

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@objectmeasurer.app.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- Email: privacy@objectmeasurer.app
